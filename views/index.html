About Us

We aim to create tailor-made education solutions by reading, researching, recording, and teaching children.

Draglearn: Born from a passion for education. Two former students, once struggling, now driven to make a difference.

Witnessing the plight of kids lacking tailored lessons ignited our vision: personalized learning for every child.

We're dedicated to revolutionizing education, which is why we've carefully developed Draglearn specifically with you and your child in mind, beginning with Grade 3.

We believe every child deserves the best, and our platform is inclusive, catering to each individual's needs.

Join us in our mission to revolutionize education and shape a brighter future for our country by offering tailored learning experiences for all.
